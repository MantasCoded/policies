# Privacy Policy

**Advectory — Analytics & Campaign Management Platform**
**Last updated: March 2, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Advectory ("we", "our", "us") operates an analytics and campaign management platform at [https://app.advectory.com](https://app.advectory.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an Advectory account, we collect your email address and name for authentication and account management purposes.

### 2.2 Third-Party Platform Data
When you connect advertising platforms (such as Meta/Facebook, Google Ads, or TikTok Ads), we access the following data through their official APIs with your explicit authorization:

- **Ad account information**: account ID, account name, currency, timezone
- **Campaign data**: campaign names, status, objectives, budgets
- **Performance metrics**: impressions, clicks, spend, conversions, reach, CTR, CPC, CPM
- **Ad creative metadata**: ad names, ad set configurations

We only access data that is necessary to provide our campaign management and analytics services.

### 2.3 OAuth Tokens
When you authorize a third-party platform, we receive and securely store OAuth access tokens. These tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM encryption before being stored in our database.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Display your advertising campaign data and performance metrics within the Advectory dashboard
- Sync campaign data between your ad platform accounts and Advectory
- Enable campaign management actions (create, pause, activate campaigns)
- Provide analytics, reporting, and performance comparisons

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data or advertising data with any third parties.

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored using Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest
- OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage
- All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Access to user data is restricted to authenticated users only via role-based access control

## 5. Data Retention

- Your data is retained for as long as your account is active
- When you disconnect a platform integration, associated campaign data and insights are deleted immediately
- You may request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us

## 6. Third-Party Services

Advectory integrates with the following third-party advertising platforms:

- **Meta (Facebook/Instagram)** — governed by [Meta's Data Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/)
- **Google Ads** — governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **TikTok Ads** — governed by [TikTok's Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy)

We access these platforms only through their official APIs and only with permissions you explicitly grant during the OAuth authorization flow.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the data we hold about you
- Disconnect any third-party platform integration at any time
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- Revoke platform access tokens at any time through the respective platform's settings

## 8. Cookies

Advectory uses essential cookies for authentication and session management only. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
